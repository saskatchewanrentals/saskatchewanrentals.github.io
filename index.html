Apartments For Rent in LeRoy Humboldt and Lanigan

We offer the finest rental units in the Humboldt region. Our company's goal is to provide the highest construction quality and comfort level so that you get a restful night's sleep after a hard day's work.

Our units are located in LeRoy Saskatchewan, 30 minutes from Humboldt and 90 minutes from Saskatoon. We are also close to Lanigan.##

All our units are 2 bedroom, 1 washroom suites. These beautiful suites have 6 appliances including in-suite washer and dryer. Our units also have air conditioning, intelligent 3-zone heating, and a beautiful freestanding fireplace.

A parking spot is included with all our units, and additional parking spots can be obtained. All our suites can be equipped with high speed internet and premium satellite television. Our units come furnished or unfurnished, please contact us about your specific needs.

We rent to individuals simply looking for one suite, or companies looking to house several workers in the region. Single night, multiple night, short term and full year leases are all available based on demand.
